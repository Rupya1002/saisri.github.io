ABOUT APSDMA

Andhra Pradesh State Disaster Management Authority(APSDMA) is the apex body in the state for Disaster Management which was constitued in 2007 as per the provisions of the Disaster Management Act, 2005 and Andhra Pradesh State Disaster Management Rules,2007.
